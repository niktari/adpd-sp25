Assignments

  • Submit a Website: Raw HTML

    Submit 1-2 raw HTML websites or websites that have predominantly default browser styles to the Teams discussion post. Include a sentence or to about how default styles are being used as a design element in your comment.

  • Book, Part 2: Start with HTML

    Translate your site map into a working website by setting up your code environment and HTML files. Populate each file with its respective content based on your site map and content organization. Focus on structure, semantics, and hierarchy. This stage is content-only—don’t worry about styling yet. Remember, no images! This is a type-only assignment.

    Consider the navigation as you're linking the pages together. For instance, can the reader only go forwards, or can they also go backwards in the narrative?

    This assignment is worth 5 out of 25 points of Project 1: Accessible Book. To get full credit, you must hit all of the requirements below.
