  • Accessible Book, Part 1: Concepts & Site Maps

    Choose an existing piece of text you'd like to work with. It can be a short story, a poem, an essay—anything you'd like to reimagine. Using pen and paper, create a site map: a visualization of the structure and navigation of your website. Think about how you want to retell the text you’ve chosen and how you can take advantage of the non-linear potential of the web to create a new experience for the reader. How many pages will you need? In what order should the user navigate those pages?

    This assignment is worth 5 out of 25 points of Project 1: Accessible Book. To get full credit, you must hit all of the requirements below.

    1. I submitted a PDF of my site map on 1/30 before 6 PM ET.

    2. I chose an existing text to work with.

    3. My site map reflects all of the pages of my website, which has at least five pages.

    4. My site map is properly annotated.

    5. My site map reflects an interesting way to re-tell the story I’ve chosen.
