  • Poster, Part 2: Refinement & Interaction

    Based on feedback, choose one stylesheet and push your concept and design in that direction.

    Add a layer of interactivity to your poster. Consider how the interaction evokes the themes of the event.

    Per usual, we will conduct our critiques in FigJam. You will present your project from your own laptop, while your classmates follow along using a direct link to your website.

    You'll have about 5-7 minutes to present, with 2-3 minutes for feedback from your classmates. I recommend you prepare your talking points beforehand so that you can speak to your work clearly yet succinctly.

    Before next week’s class, make sure to:

    1. Paste a link to your project in your assigned crit board in this FigJam document. (Required)
    2. Add screenshots or reference images that supplement your presentation. (Optional)

    This assignment is worth 4 out of 8 points of Project 2: Poster. To get full credit, you must hit all of the requirements below.

  • Extra Credit: Flexbox Letterforms

    Explore the use of Flexbox to create gridded letterforms. You can choose a word, create an alphabet, or go crazy on one letter. If you want to lean into pixelation, great. If you want to create more organic letterforms, consider what other CSS properties you can use to break up the rigidity of the grid. Maybe border-radius? Softer colors? Shapes that are not just squares and circles? What kind of interaction, like :hover, can you add? How does it add meaning to the word or letter? Play around!

    Please be sure to study these CodePens for more reinforcement:

    1. Study Example: Responsive Flexbox Grid
    2. Study Example: Flexbox Word

    Feel free to work in a CodePen or a folder within your class site.
